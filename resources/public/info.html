Canvas Rules

We pride ourselves on trying to keep an open canvas for all free from outside interference on our end, and especially censorship. However, for the good of the community and on accounts of our own beliefs, please acknowledge and obey the following guidelines:

  1. No hateful imagery or derogatory speech. This includes but is not limited to words such as f*ggot, n*gger, etc; as well as the Swastika, Hammer and Sickle, and any symbols of terrorism.
  2. The general rating for artwork is PG-13.
  3. No NSFW or NSFL content.
    • No nudity or otherwise sexually explicit content
    • No sexual imagery/erotica
    • No excessive blood or otherwise obscene/shocking content
  4. No more than one account per user, no exceptions. Multiple account users will be banned from creating art on the canvas.
  5. No auto-placement tools of any kind, you must place the pixels manually.
  6. Do not abuse site functionality, such as reports or lookups. (e.g. automated reporting/lookups, etc.)
  7. Any automated aggregation of data from user lookups is not allowed, and will result in a ban.
  8. Staff have final say in any rule disputes
      If you feel a moderator has acted inappropriately, please report it to an administrator.

If you believe you have been falsely banned you may contact a moderator or administrator.

Chat Rules

  1. Keep chat civil. No harassment or homophobic/transphobic/disablist language.
  2. No hate speech. This includes emoji/symbols/ASCII art.
    • Normal swearing/etc is allowed
  3. No spamming
    • This includes excessive ASCII art/emojis/symbols/whitespace
  4. No "copy pasta"s
  5. No links to sites that actively break the canvas or chat rules (e.g. porn sites)
  6. No symbology which break the canvas or chat rules (e.g. NSFW ASCII art)
  7. No personal information
  8. Staff have final say in any rule disputes
    • If you feel a moderator has acted inappropriately, please report it to an administrator.
